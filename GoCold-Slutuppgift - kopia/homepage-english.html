Discover the Nordic mountains

Trysil

Not far north of Oslo you will find the nice ski resort Trysil which has a large selection of hotels to offer. The ski resort offers both downhill and cross-country skiing and is perfect for the family.

Sälen

Sälen is one of Sweden's most famous ski resorts and consists of the four ski areas Lindvallen, Högfjället, Tandådalen and Hundfjället.

Branäs

Skiing, gym or cross-country skiing. In Branäs, there are opportunities for training in several ways. Out on the slopes you will also find nine fun snow activities to try!

Hemsedal

Hemsedal has a wide selection of cottages, large houses and apartments and most are located near ski slopes and lifts. There are also two large SkiStar Lodge for those seeking extra comfort.

Åre

Discover new parts of skiing in Åre together with professional guides and instructors. Here are experiences both for you who want to find hidden off-piste gems and for you who want to go on a trip with the family.
